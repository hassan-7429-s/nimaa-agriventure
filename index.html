Who We Are

We firmly believe we have proven solutions to sustainably produce fresh & tasty food. The key is not only increasing production, but minimizing environmental impact and making local greenhouses profitable.

  • Pioneer spirit: new greenhouse locations in frontier markets.
  • Global expertise: FVEC in Netherlands plus local execution worldwide.
  • Hands-on expatriate growers for local team mentor support.

Technology

Proven, state-of-the-art technologies developed in cooperation with the Dutch greenhouse ecosystem.

Controlled Environment Agriculture (CEA)

  • Advanced climate control and LED grow lighting for year-round production.
  • Hybrid heating/cooling sources with low environmental footprint.
  • Hydroponic growing, precision irrigation, biological pest control, and GlobalGAP certification.

Current Operations

Proven success in frontier markets made us ambitious to grow. Our approach covers feasibility, design, construction, operations and marketing.
